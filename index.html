HEALTH AND SAFETY PLAN WORKSHEET

This form can be used to generate a standard S&W Site Specific Health and Safety Plan and Job Safety Analysis (JSA) Worksheet (required by Shannon & Wilson for field activities) or an Activity Hazard Analysis (AHA)—typically required for government projects.

This form will generate a JSA/AHA for common tasks only.  Additional input into the JSA/AHA will be required for activity steps unique to your project.

After you have completed the SSHSP and JSA/AHA, the project manager or site safety manager must discuss all activity steps, hazards, and controls with the field staff.
